Annual Development Levy Collection Notice

New Healthcare Facility Opens in Osi-Ekiti

We are pleased to announce the official opening of the Osi-Ekiti Community Health Center, a state-of-the-art medical facility that will provide quality healthcare services to our residents. The facility is equipped with modern medical equipment and staffed by qualified healthcare professionals.

Services include general consultation, maternal and child health, immunization, laboratory services, and emergency care. The center operates 24/7 to ensure our community has access to healthcare when needed.

Congratulations to Dr. Adebayo Ogundimu

The entire Osi-Ekiti community joins in celebrating Dr. Adebayo Ogundimu, son of our soil, on his recent appointment as the Director of Medical Services at the Federal Medical Centre, Ido-Ekiti.
